About me

Hi, I'm Khaled Hazime, a 21 years old student from Brazil.
I'm currently studying Computer Engineering at the University Center of FEI.
I'm also a member of the RoboFEI@Home team, a team that develops robots for the RoboCup@Home competition.

I have been working with ROS since 2018 and have gained considerable expertise in this field. I also had the opportunity to participate in the Latin American Robotics Competition in the same year, which was a great learning experience for me. More recently, in 2021, I participated in the RoboCup@Home competition, which allowed me to showcase my skills and compete with some of the best minds in the field of robotics. These experiences have helped me develop my skills and knowledge in the field of robotics, and I am excited to continue exploring and contributing to this fascinating field.
